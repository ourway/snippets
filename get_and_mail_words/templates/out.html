hello

Definition

  1. used as a greeting or response, as in telephoning
  2. an instance of saying or exclaiming “hello”
  3. to say or exclaim “hello” (to)

Synonyms

  • good afternoon
  • good evening

Examples

  • The priest usually remembered D'Amelio's name and would greet him with a casual " hello.

welcome

ˈwɛlkəm

Definition

  2. an act or expression of welcoming
  3. you are welcome here
  4. to greet with pleasure and hospitality

Synonyms

  • greet
  • acceptable
  • salutation
  • hospitality
  • hail

Examples

beam

bim

Definition

  1. the squared-off trunk of a tree
  2. a long, thick piece of wood, metal, or stone, used in building
  3. a shaft or stream of light or other radiation, as of X-rays or nuclear particles
  4. to give out (shafts of light); radiate in a beam or beams
  5. to shine brightly; be radiant

Synonyms

  • glow
  • send out
  • glint
  • grin
  • spar
  • support

Examples

  • One man is shown braced against a support beam in the crush with his mouth wide open, his arms in the air.
  • The jug skidded across the floor, came to rest against a support beam.
